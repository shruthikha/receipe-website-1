Fish and Chips

A British classic! Crispy battered fish served with golden fries and tartar sauce â€“ a satisfying and timeless meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 white fish fillets (cod/haddock)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cold soda water or beer
  • Potatoes for fries
  • Oil for frying
  • Lemon wedges & tartar sauce to serve

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thick fries. Soak in water for 30 mins.
  2. Pat fish dry. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and soda water into a batter.
  3. Heat oil and fry potatoes until golden and crispy. Drain and set aside.
  4. Dip fish in batter and fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Serve hot with fries, lemon wedges, and tartar sauce.
