Masala Chai

Warm and comforting Indian tea made with black tea leaves, spices, and milk. A soul-soothing experience in every sip.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp black tea leaves
  • 1 inch ginger, crushed
  • 2-3 crushed cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 1-2 tsp sugar (to taste)

Instructions

  1. In a pot, add water, spices, and bring to a boil.
  2. Add tea leaves and simmer for 2â€“3 minutes.
  3. Add milk and sugar, then bring to boil again.
  4. Strain into cups and serve hot.
