Shepherd's Pie

A classic British comfort food made with minced meat, vegetables, and topped with creamy mashed potatoes, baked until golden.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes
  • Salt, pepper, herbs

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions, carrots in oil. Add meat and cook until browned.
  2. Stir in tomato paste, peas, salt, and pepper. Cook 5 more minutes.
  3. Spread the mixture in a baking dish and top with mashed potatoes.
  4. Bake at 200°C (390°F) for 25–30 minutes until golden brown.
