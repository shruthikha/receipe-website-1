Aloo Paratha

A classic North Indian breakfast! Aloo Paratha is a crispy, flaky flatbread stuffed with a spiced potato filling. Served with butter, yogurt, or pickles – it’s comforting, filling, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough with flour, water, and a pinch of salt. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Mash potatoes and mix with spices and coriander.
  3. Roll dough, stuff with potato mix, and seal.
  4. Roll again gently into flat rounds.
  5. Cook on hot tawa using ghee until golden brown on both sides.
