Grilled Steak

A juicy, perfectly grilled steak seasoned with salt, pepper, and garlic – simple yet flavorful. Best enjoyed with veggies or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye or sirloin steaks
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme (optional)

Instructions

  1. Season steaks with salt, pepper, garlic, and herbs. Let rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat grill or skillet over high heat. Add oil.
  3. Grill each side for 3–5 minutes depending on thickness and doneness.
  4. Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
