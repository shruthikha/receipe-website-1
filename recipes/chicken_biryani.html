Chicken Biryani

Aromatic basmati rice cooked with marinated chicken, spices, and herbs. A royal treat packed with flavor and tradition.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, marinated
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves)
  • Fresh coriander and mint
  • Ghee and oil
  • Saffron milk

Instructions

  1. Fry onions until golden and set aside.
  2. Cook marinated chicken with spices until tender.
  3. Parboil basmati rice with whole spices.
  4. Layer rice and chicken in a pot with herbs and saffron milk.
  5. Cover tightly and cook on low for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with raita or salad.
