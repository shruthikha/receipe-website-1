Palak Paneer

A flavorful Indian vegetarian dish made with puréed spinach and paneer cubes cooked with spices. Creamy, nutritious, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 250g spinach leaves (washed and blanched)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala, salt, turmeric

Instructions

  1. Blanch spinach, cool and blend to a puree.
  2. Sauté cumin and onions until golden, add ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook till oil separates.
  4. Add spinach puree and paneer. Simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  5. Finish with cream or butter (optional) and serve hot.
