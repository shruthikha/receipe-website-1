Iced Coffee

A cold and refreshing coffee beverage that’s smooth and energizing — perfect for summer mornings!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed coffee (cooled)
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • 1-2 tsp sugar (to taste)
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: whipped cream or syrup

Instructions

  1. Brew coffee and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour in coffee and milk/cream.
  4. Add sugar and stir well.
  5. Top with whipped cream or syrup if desired.
