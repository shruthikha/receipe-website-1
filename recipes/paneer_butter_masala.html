Paneer Butter Masala

Rich, creamy and luscious Indian curry made with paneer cubes simmered in a buttery tomato-based gravy. Perfect with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: cumin, chili powder, garam masala, kasuri methi
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic paste in butter until golden.
  2. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  3. Add paneer cubes and mix gently.
  4. Pour in cream, simmer for 5–10 minutes on low heat.
  5. Garnish with kasuri methi and serve hot.
