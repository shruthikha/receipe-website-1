Rajma Chawal

A hearty and comforting North Indian dish made with red kidney beans simmered in a spiced tomato gravy, served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rajma (red kidney beans)
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: cumin, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak rajma overnight and pressure cook until soft.
  2. In a pan, sauté onions and ginger-garlic paste until golden.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add cooked rajma and simmer for 20–25 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice.
