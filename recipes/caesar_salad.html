Caesar Salad

A light and flavorful salad made with romaine lettuce, croutons, parmesan cheese, and creamy Caesar dressing. Perfect as a side or a healthy main dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp Caesar dressing
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Optional: grilled chicken or anchovies

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce thoroughly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, croutons, and parmesan.
  3. Drizzle with Caesar dressing and lemon juice.
  4. Toss until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Top with grilled chicken or anchovies if desired and serve chilled.
