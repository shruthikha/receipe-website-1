Chole Bhature

A popular North Indian street food combo – spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with fluffy deep-fried bread (bhature).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • Chole masala, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin, salt
  • 2 cups flour, 2 tbsp curd, 1/2 tsp baking soda (for bhature)

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook chickpeas until soft. Set aside.
  2. Sauté onions and ginger garlic paste until golden. Add tomatoes and spices.
  3. Add chickpeas and cook 15 mins on low flame. Garnish with coriander.
  4. Mix flour, curd, baking soda. Knead to a soft dough and rest 2 hrs.
  5. Roll into discs and deep-fry until puffed and golden.
