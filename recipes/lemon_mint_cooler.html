Lemon Mint Cooler

A refreshing summer drink with zesty lemon, cooling mint, and a touch of sweetness to beat the heat!

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tsp sugar or honey
  • 1 cup chilled soda or water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Muddle mint leaves with sugar in a glass.
  2. Add lemon juice and fill the glass with ice.
  3. Pour chilled soda or water over the top.
  4. Stir well and garnish with a mint sprig.
