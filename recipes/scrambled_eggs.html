Scrambled Eggs

Creamy, fluffy scrambled eggs made with simple ingredients and cooked to perfection. A classic breakfast staple that never goes out of style!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk or cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl. Add milk, salt, and pepper. Whisk until smooth.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  4. Stir gently and continuously with a spatula until curds begin to form.
  5. Remove from heat while still slightly wet (theyâ€™ll finish cooking off heat).
  6. Garnish with chives and serve hot.
