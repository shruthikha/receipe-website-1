Dinner Recipes

Wind down with delicious, satisfying dinners from Indian comfort foods to global favorites – perfect to end your day!

Dal Makhani

Rich and creamy black lentils slow-cooked with butter, cream, and aromatic spices – a North Indian classic.

Palak Paneer

Soft paneer cubes simmered in a velvety spinach gravy seasoned with garlic, cumin, and garam masala.

Spicy chickpea curry paired with fluffy deep-fried bhature – the ultimate Punjabi street-style dinner!

Shepherd's Pie

British-style baked casserole of minced meat and vegetables topped with creamy mashed potatoes.

Grilled Steak

Perfectly grilled steak seasoned with herbs, garlic butter, and served with roasted veggies or fries.
