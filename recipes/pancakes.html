Pancakes

Fluffy, golden pancakes are the perfect breakfast treat. Top them with maple syrup, fruits, or whipped cream to make them extra special!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir just until combined (do not overmix).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  5. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side until golden.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, fruits, or your favorite toppings.
