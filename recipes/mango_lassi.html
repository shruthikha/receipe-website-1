Mango Lassi

A chilled, creamy mango yogurt drink perfect for beating the heat. Sweet, tangy, and absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango chunks
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tsp sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/8 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add mango, yogurt, milk, sugar, and cardamom into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes if desired. Blend again briefly.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve chilled.
