Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a crispy South Indian crepe filled with a spicy potato mixture and served with coconut chutney and sambar. It’s golden, crispy, and packed with flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter (fermented)
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chili, and onions. Sauté until translucent.
  2. Add turmeric, mashed potatoes, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Heat a non-stick tawa. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter and spread thin in a circular motion.
  4. Drizzle oil around edges. Cook until golden and crispy.
  5. Place some potato filling in the center and fold the dosa. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
